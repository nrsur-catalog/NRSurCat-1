NRSurrogate Catalog#

We present the posterior samples of the 47 GW events (from 2015-2020, LVK O1-O3) with signal durations less than 64 seconds from the NRSurrogate Catalog [1]. The catalog fits use the NRSur7dq4 [2] surrogate model. For details on the analysis, please refer to the catalog paper.

Main Dependencies

All fits were performed with ❤️ using:

  • NRSur7dq4 [2]

  • bilby [3]

  • dynesty [4]
